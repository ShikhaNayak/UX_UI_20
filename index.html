Redesign-Department of Labor
  • User Research (current)
  • Ideation
  • Prototype

About the Project :

Goal : The Goal of this project was to redesign U.S Department Of Labor website for a more modern and simplistic look which will help our users to navigate and search the necessary information efficiently.

Problem : Overwhelming amounts of information in the website was making it way more difficult for the users to navigate through the website and get the needed information.

MY ROLE : User Experience & Interface Design, User Research

Disciplines & Tools :User Research (Interviews) & Analysis, Interaction Design, Wireframing, Prototyping Tools : Figma, Miro & Photoshop

Heuristic-Evaluation :

> Homepage is not very explanatory as what the department covers.

> The Website colors are blind Safe.

> There is too much of information but the path to get the information is very long or hard to find.

> Colors are simple easy colors but some of the buttons does not look like button.

> In the Forms section they have too many forms and categories too but, the categories and far down and hard to find.

User Scenario and Usability test tasks :

As a UX designer we want to know about how a user who recently got hurt at the workplace will be able to find more information about reimbursement of medical bills and leave benefits. Task 1 : Find medical Reimbursement form. Task 2 : Find Employee Serious Health Condition Form
